ABOUT US

We are a start-up, founded by a quartet of physicists aims to dedicate itself for the development of quantum and quantum-enabling technologies. We utilize quantum physics, electronics, optics, and vacuum technologies to build real-time hardware, quantum sensors, quantum computers and simulators.

Ranging from defense to civil applications today quantum technology has made its relevance in every aspect of life by exploiting the quantum nature of matter. We develop products for quantum sensing related to cold atoms using cooling and trapping them. Also, this feature of trapping atoms is also being used to develop neutral atom quantum computers to solve complex problems such as material designing, portfolio optimization, drug modeling, logistic optimization, etc.
