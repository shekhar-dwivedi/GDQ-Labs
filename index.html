ABOUT US

We commenced our journey with a group of four physicists aboard for indigenous development of quantum and quantum-enabling technology (on Indian soil). We believe that our endeavour will extend inordinate advantages of quantum technology to the nation in the long run.

Quantum technology promises potentially unmatched solutions to a wide variety of problems. For instance, the gradual saturation of the modern computational resources to its fundamental limit may be overcome by means of developing simulators/computers powered by the quantum phenomena of the nature. Measuring different physical quantities with the supreme accuracy is another possibility that may be achieved using quantum technology enhancing the performance of our positioning devices (GPS), exponentiating the prediction accuracy of natural disasters (earthquakes) and many more.
